About Me

As graphic design student with a passion for Layout, UX/UI design, and Illustration it is always important to think of goals and plan ahead.

I look at graphic design as communication, meaning that the work has to have a vibe to connect to the viewer or perceiver. I make a black and white drawing and then add color digitally, bringing in a contemporary pattern to the composition to create a vibrance.
          -John Van Hamersveld

Energetic and self-motivated graphic designer seeking a position to use creative skills to communicate products and services to customers using good time management, creative flair, and attention to detail.

Short-Term:

To obtain an internship in a creative, but structured environment. Achieving this will challenge and grow my graphic design skills and allow me to make connections with other designers.

To be hired as Graphic Designer at a corporation that helps discover creative ways to broaden their branding. I would like to work with branding and layouts to help them market to their new expanded audience, and create a whole new perception for their company.
