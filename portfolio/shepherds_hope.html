Shepherd’s Hope

This was a rebranding class project with Shepherd's Hope for their 25th Anniversary Masquerade “Night at the Museum” Event.

"Caring People, Caring for People"

Brand Strategy: In celebration of their 25th Anniversary Masquerade “Night at the Museum” Event I designed posters based on their 'exhibitions' that will be premiered that night.

SHEPHERD'S HOPE DESCRIPTIONS

Each sign for the event are staged as "exhibitions" and therefore should be viewed as such. Sized at 24"x36 (standard poster size) there are 8 total exhibitions, a Mission statement sign, Sponsor signs, Stationary & Packaging, Event Invitation, Attendee Badge & Lanyard, and Swag such as buttons, hats, bags, calendar, and coffee cups.

    The exhibitions are as follows:
  • Ancient Athens
  • Famous Artists
  • Buda
  • Mayan
  • Ancient Egypt
  • African Safari
  • Dinosaurs
  • Rockets
