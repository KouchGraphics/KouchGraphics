Pemburton's Whiskey Alliance

This was a class project that was rebranding Pemburton's Whiskey Alliance.

What the restaurant is looking for in their brand:
- A high quality look that’s creative, trendy, and fun, but professional too
- Capture the aestethic of the speakeasy era with typography and color

Libations and Burgers
Orlando, FL & Historic Sanford, FL
PRICE RANGE

Business Description:
Most people consider a beer to be a burgers best compliment but Pemburton’s wants to make people consider a fine cocktail to be a burger’s best friend. Featuring custom mixed cocktails that pair well with exotic flame grilled burgers / pub food. A slightly different take on the “gastropub”. The location has a distinct speakeasy vibe with antique wood, mustachesporting hipster bartenders, and stained glass.

Who are their Clients:
Individuals who want to look classy and high-end, but not so high-end that they come across as elitist. People who want to enjoy exotic and unexpected evenings that they can immerse themselves in.
