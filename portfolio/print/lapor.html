Lapor

This is an individual project that is important to me; Lapor which means lips in Spanish is a fictional organization that is trying to fight human trafficking. Lapor is one of the many organizations that fight human trafficking today.

The reason Lapor was chosen as a name is because many people in the fight human trafficking industry cannot speak out, therefore it is up to everyone else outside of the industry to speak up for them.

Lapor is not a real organization but is based off real organizations that fight human trafficking.
