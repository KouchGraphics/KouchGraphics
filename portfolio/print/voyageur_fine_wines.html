Voyageur Fine Wines

This was a UX/UI class project that was branding Voyageur Fine Wines.

A small franchise in Southern California, Voyageur sells a variety wine and wine accessories. They’ve had a small online presence locally via social media, but they have no website.

Goals:
Their short term goal is to branch out to sell nationwide, with a long term goal of expanding to international sales.

BUSINESS DESCRIPTIONS

What are the top 3 goals of the client for this project?
1. Create a Website, branching out to all of California and the states surrounding it
2. To branch out to sell nationwide
3. Expand to international sales

Business Description:
Voyageur Fine Wines is a business that sells wine and wine accessories. They have been around for 10 years building up their vineyard and creating their special wine through breeding, for the best tasting result with no special additives. Until now, they have been only able to sell local to California, but due to their growing demands they would like to spread their industry to everyone in the Nation. The founder and CEO Caleb Dounger explains that he created the company because he felt no one could make wine as good as his father. His father used to make his own wine from grapes he grew in their family garden and would make one bottle of wine a year for special occasions. However, Caleb felt everyone should have a taste of his father’s famous wine and thus the company was born.

Who are their Clients:
Individuals who want to look classy and high-end, but not so high-end that they come across as elitist. People who want to enjoy exotic and unexpected evenings that they can immerse themselves in.
